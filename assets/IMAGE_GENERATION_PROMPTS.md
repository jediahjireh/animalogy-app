# Animalogy — Image Generation Prompts

## Global Style Guidelines (Apply to All)

- **Resolution**: All images at 2x (minimum 640px on shortest edge for avatars, 1200px wide for scenes)
- **Style**: Children's storybook cartoon — bold outlines (2-3px), soft gradients, rounded shapes, vibrant saturated colors. Think Bluey/Octonauts/Wild Kratts aesthetic, NOT anime or photorealistic
- **Character Design**: Expressive, anthropomorphic animals with large eyes, friendly proportions. Animals stand on four legs (not bipedal) but with human-readable facial expressions
- **Color Palette**: Warm earth tones as base — sandy golds, forest greens, ocean teals — with each region having its own accent palette (see per-region notes)
- **Mood**: Safe, warm, inviting. Educational but never scary. Villains look sneaky/sly, never horrifying
- **Rendering**: Clean vector-style with soft cel shading. Subtle texture overlays (paper grain or watercolor edges) welcome for warmth
- **Background**: Transparent PNG for mascot avatars. Full scene for story illustrations and pack covers
- **Avoid**: Realistic proportions, sharp/angular art styles, dark horror aesthetics, text/words in the image, emojis, overly complex/cluttered compositions, human characters
